Katakana

Katakana (カタカナ) is a Japanese script primarily used for writing foreign words, names, onomatopoeia, and technical or scientific terms.

It is one of the three main scripts in Japanese, alongside Hiragana (ひらがな) and Kanji (漢字).

The Katakana script is primarily used for loanwords (words borrowed from other languages), technical terms, and names of foreign places or people.
